Save the Bees!

"If the bee disappeared off the face of the earth, man would only have four years left to live."

Maurice Maeterlinck, The Life of the Bee

Scientists fear the honeybee disappearance presents a profound threat to humans' food supply along with an undetermined ecological impact. The Department of Agriculture estimates that honeybees pollinate about one-third of the nation's diet and account for $15 billion in U.S. crop production. Bees are vital to our survival and they need our help!
